Dance

If you love to dance and perform, come be a part of the Dance Team! This is a great place to work on your dance technique and choreography skills. Students are given many opportunities to showcase their talents in multiple different ways. They will have the chance to dance in the Spirit of Waterloo football game, assemblies, The Arts and Culture Festival, and compete at multiple competitions such as Dance Canada and Strive.

❮ ❯

More about WCI Dance

The dance team coaches are Ms. McGonegal and Ms. Urquhart. Once the season starts in September, look out for posters and announcements around the school on where to join or email the coaches directly. Dance team runs all year starting in September and ending in May and meets every Wednesday and Friday at lunch in the annex and/or KT gym (depending on the time of year and what dance you are preparing for, the schedule may vary with potential morning practices and/or longer committed practices outside of school hours).
